"locked in" means ==to enter a state of intense focus and concentration on a specific task, goal, or activity, with all distractions removed==. It signifies a high level of determination and being "in the zone" to achieve something, whether it's studying for a test, practicing a sport, playing a video game, or working on a personal project. 

- **Intense focus:**
    
    It's not just casual concentration; it's a complete immersion in the task at hand. 
    

- **Purpose-driven:**
    
    It's used when a person is deliberately setting aside distractions to put all their energy into a goal.
